Timez Attack

Great new FREE! multiplication game.

Step 1. Go to www.bigbrainz.com and click on the choice for School Use.

Step 2. Select either Windows or Mac to download. Follow the prompts to Run, as well as the Set Up prompts.

Step 3. The program will launch and the Timez Attakk icon will appear on your desktop. Click Finish and then wait for the program to Download and Unpack.

Step 4. The Timez Attack Launcher will come up; select School and Paid when prompted

Step 5. Next, select the Country, State, Country, School and password information. Your child's password is sles (all lowercase).

Step 6. After you log-in, select the students's teacher (class) name; then select the student's name.
